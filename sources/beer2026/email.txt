Thanks for your submission. I really appreciate that you took the time to validate the files per the instructions! Feedback after a quick review:

borehole.csv

* Columns date_min, date_max are the dates of drilling, not measurement. Please revise.
* Please fill out column ice_depth with the starting depth of continuous ice at each borehole, in the same depth datum as reported for the measurements. For example, if you drilled into bare ice, you probably defined depth relative to this upper ice surface at the time of installation, and so ice_depth=0. If snow/firn was present and you defined depth relative to the start of ice, ice_depth=0 (but mention depth of snow/firn in the notes). If snow/firn was present and you defined depth relative to the upper surface at time of installation, ice_depth=snow/firn depth.
* If you have estimates of ice ablation during the period of measurement (or anything that changes the depth of the temperature measurements relative to the time-varying surface), please include that in the notes.

measurement.csv

* What is the resolution of the temperature measurements? Your file uses 7+ digits after the period (which increases the file size), but I presume the true precision is around 2–3 digits?
